# NGM Court Identifier Codes

Used with `ngm_extract_case_data` and `ngm_query_judicial` tools.

## Supreme & Special Courts

- `supreme` — Supreme Court (सर्वोच्च अदालत)
- `special` — Special Court (विशेष अदालत)

## District Court Identifier Format

Format: `<district>dc`

Example: `kathmandudc`, `lalitpurdc`, `pokharadc`, `chitwandc`

Use lowercase, remove spaces and special characters. Common abbreviations are used for districts with compound names.
