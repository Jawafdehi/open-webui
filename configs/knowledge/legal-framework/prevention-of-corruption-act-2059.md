# Prevention of Corruption Act, 2059 (2002)

**Act No. 17 of the year 2059 (2002)**

## Overview

The Prevention of Corruption Act is Nepal's primary anti-corruption legislation. It defines corruption offenses, establishes the CIAA's investigative powers, and sets penalties for corrupt acts.

*Note: Full legal text to be sourced from the Nepal Law Commission (www.lawcommission.gov.np) and uploaded here.*

## Key Provisions

### Definitions (Section 2)
- Defines "public servant," "corruption," "gratification," "property"

### Offenses (Sections 3-12)
- Bribery and gratification
- Illegal property acquisition
- Abuse of authority
- False claims and documents
- Possession of disproportionate assets

### Investigation (Sections 13-20)
- CIAA powers to investigate
- Search and seizure
- Asset freezing
- Witness examination

### Penalties (Sections 3-12)
- Imprisonment terms
- Fines
- Property confiscation
- Disqualification from public office

### Special Provisions
- Burden of proof on accused for disproportionate assets
- Statute of limitations
- International cooperation
