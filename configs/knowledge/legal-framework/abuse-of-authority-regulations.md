# Abuse of Authority Regulations

## Overview

Regulations issued under the CIAA Act, 2048 and the Prevention of Corruption Act, 2059 that provide detailed procedural rules for investigation and prosecution of corruption cases.

*Note: Full legal text to be sourced from the Nepal Law Commission and uploaded here.*

## Key Areas

### Investigation Regulations
- Complaint handling procedures
- Evidence collection standards
- Witness interview protocols
- Search warrant procedures
- Asset seizure procedures

### Asset Declaration
- Public officials required to declare assets
- Timeline and format for declarations
- Verification procedures
- Consequences for false declarations

### Public Procurement Oversight
- CIAA oversight of government contracts
- Thresholds for mandatory CIAA review
- Red flag indicators in procurement
- Contractor blacklisting procedures

### Whistleblower Protection
- Protection of informants
- Confidentiality guarantees
- Anti-retaliation provisions
- Reward mechanisms

## Related Bodies

- **National Vigilance Centre (NVC)**: Preventive anti-corruption body
- **Office of the Auditor General (OAG)**: Financial audits of government
- **Public Procurement Monitoring Office (PPMO)**: Procurement oversight
