# Identifying Corruption Patterns

## Common Corruption Types in Nepal

### Procurement Fraud
- Bid rigging and collusion in government tenders
- Inflated contract values
- Substandard materials in public works
- Ghost projects (paid for but never built)

### Financial Embezzlement
- Misappropriation of government funds
- Fake receipts and forged documents
- Double billing or duplicate payments
- Salary fraud (ghost employees)

### Public Service Corruption
- Bribery for service delivery (passports, licenses, permits)
- Nepotism and cronyism in public appointments
- Undue influence in hiring and promotions
- Commission/kickback schemes

### Land and Property Fraud
- Illegal land transfers of public property
- Fake land ownership documents
- Encroachment on public land
- Undervaluation in property registration

## Red Flags to Watch For

- Unexplained wealth disproportionate to known income
- Lifestyle inconsistent with declared assets
- Frequent foreign travel without clear purpose
- Complex corporate structures with opaque ownership
- Relatives holding positions in contracting companies
- Pattern of single-bidder tenders
- Rapid turnover in key procurement positions

## Using Chat Tools for Pattern Detection

- `search_cases`: Search for similar cases by sector/tag
- `search_entities`: Cross-reference persons across multiple cases
- `extract_court_data`: Check court history for repeated appearances
